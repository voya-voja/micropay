Benefits to Financial Institutions from MicroAc© Service

The iNexum© approach to micropayments was created with the unique needs of banks in mind. Banks are active participants creating and controlling their MicroAc© accounts. MicroAc builds on retail banking security and trust leveraging the banks' brands to accelerate consumer and merchant adoption rates.

The MicroAc system extends online banking capabilities by providing consumers and merchants with one simple, convenient and cost effective method of payment for traditional and digital microproducts, for local and global transactions.

MicroAc is a unique solution that can support many user interfaces - bankcards, Internet browsers, wireless devices, smartcards - from the same bank account. These can be rolled out separately or integrated together into a full micropayment service offering.

Unlock the power of your existing customer relationships with iNexum's MicroAc micropayment solutions.

Some of the benefits to banks from iNexum's MicroAc solution are:

New and additional revenues:

  • Processing fees from traditional retail transactions
  • Transaction processing fees from new microproduct markets
  • Foreign exchange earnings from international transactions

Improved cost control:

  • One banking product, many access points.
  • Cash maintenance cost reduction.
  • No changes to the existing e-payment infrastructure. MicroAc supports widely adopted financial message and batch file formats.
  • Micropayment gateway built on top of existing acquiring and issuing e-payment infrastructure, thus reducing cost and time necessary for bank to incorporate electronic micropayments into existing payment model.
  • Outsourcing of processing responsibilities to iNexum, reduces cost and time necessary to setup micropayment processing facilities, train resources and maintain the system.
  • Charge-back management. MicroAc provides a full audit trail of micropayment transactions.
  • Timely transition to new technologies
  • Encapsulation of developing micropayment standards. MicroAc services protect a bank's underlying e-payment network from potential changes related to the evolution of micropayment standards.

Value-added Consumer & Merchant services:

  • Packaged financial product for broad distribution
  • Consumer/merchant retention
  • Full multi-currency support
  • Increased access to a wider consumer/merchant base
